About Me

Hi, I'm Jane! I'm a UX Designer with a background in web development. I was Baker and Pastry Chef for many years before becoming a UX Designer. I took classes at the New York Code + Design Academy in both web development and UX. I really gravitated towards UX given my degree in Psychology from SUNY New Paltz, I find learning the interaction of human behaviors towards technology fascinating. It makes me want to learn and create the best possibilities of executing enjoyable user experiences. I feel like both worlds are no different from one another, rather than creating a comforting experience where diners can enjoy a sweet treat after a meal. I create user experience for products that users can easily navigate accessibility to make their journey enjoyable.

I love creating things from the ground up. It's so exciting to see a product that you started from the beginning turn into something that go above and beyond your expectations.
