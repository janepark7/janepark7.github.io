About Me

Hi, I'm Jane! I'm a UX Designer with a background in web development. I was Baker and Pastry Chef for many years before becoming a UX Designer. I feel like both worlds are no different from one another, rather than creating a comforting experience where diners can enjoy a sweet treat after a meal. I create a comforting user experience for products that users can enjoy simple accessibility on their products.

I love creating things from the ground up. It's so exciting to see a product that you started from the beginning turn into something that go above and beyond your expectations.
