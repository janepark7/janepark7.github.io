About Me

Hi, I'm Jane! I'm a UX Designer. I was Baker and Pastry Chef for many years before becoming a UX Designer. I feel like both worlds are no different from one another, except having the end result being a sweet treat (that you can eat), you have a functioning webpage or app to display all of your work to make your life just as sweet!

I love creating things from the ground up. It's so exciting to see a product that you started from the beginning turn into something that go above and beyond your expectations. I created my first webpage when I was in high school by making fan pages for my favorite bands on Angelfire (shout out to hosting pages of the 90's). After graduating from New York Code and Design Academy's Web Development Intensive program, I have learned that creating webpages and apps can go above and beyond HTML (and hosting them on Angelfire).

Technologies that I use: HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node, React, Postgres, MongoDB, WordPress, PHP
