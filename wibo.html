About the organizations and it's Goals
I enrolled at BeginEx, a UX Design accelerated program based on real social impact projects with real clients. Our client, the Workshop in Business Opportunities “WIBO” has been around for more than 50+ years and has taught more than 18,000 business owners how to grow a sustainable business through our 16 weeks “How to Build a Growing Profitable Business” workshop.
WIBO’s mission is to enable small business owners and budding entrepreneurs from underserved communities to obtain financial success by starting, operating, and building successful businesses that develop economic power, provide jobs and improve communities. helped many people become business owners/entrepreneurs. They want to build an e-commerce platform for their alumni for them to sell and promote their products and services.
Our Challenge
Our challenge is to create an effective and intuitive way for staff to manage the platform: understand and analyze global sales results, resolve issues and communicate with business owners and customers and manage product / services pages.
My Role
I was the UX Designer + Researcher for this project in addition to being appointed as Project Manager for my team.
