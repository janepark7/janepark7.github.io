About the organizations and it's Goals
Workshop In Business Opportunities, “WIBO” has been around for more than 50+ years and has taught more than 18,000 business owners how to grow a sustainable business throughout their 16 week program on “How to Build a Growing Profitable Business” workshop. WIBO’s mission is to enable small business owners and budding entrepreneurs from underserved communities to obtain financial success by starting, operating, and building successful business that develop economic power, provide jobs and improve communities. Their plan is to build an e-commerce platform for their alumni to help them sell and promote their products and/or services.
Our Task
Our task is to create an effective and intuitive way for staff to manage the platform: understand and analyze global sales results, resolve issues and communicate with business owners and customers and manage product/services pages.
