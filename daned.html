Daniel Edelman

Project Details

My client Daniel Edelman is a video editor. He wanted a single page portfolio to showcase his video reels, in addition providing his contact information and resume. Since the primary focus of this page is to showcase his work, I determined the background should be bare. The portfolio should be straight to the point, so I formated as a giant resume. I created this site on Wordpress, which also allows him to change the content of the page. In addition, this website is also responsive on mobile.
